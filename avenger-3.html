Who is Hulk?

Hulk is a giant, green, super-strong hero created when scientist Bruce Banner was exposed to dangerous gamma radiation. Whenever Bruce gets angry or stressed, he transforms into the Incredible Hulk—a powerhouse with nearly unlimited strength.

Fun Facts:

Hulk’s famous catchphrase: “HULK SMASH!”

Though he looks scary, Hulk often tries to protect people.

In the MCU, Hulk is played by Mark Ruffalo.

Hulk wasn’t always green! In his very first comic, he was grey.


Forms of Hulk:

Savage Hulk: The classic “HULK SMASH!” version – pure rage and strength.

Professor Hulk: A mix of Bruce’s mind and Hulk’s body – smart and strong.

Did You Know?
World Breaker Hulk: An extremely powerful version capable of destroying planets.
