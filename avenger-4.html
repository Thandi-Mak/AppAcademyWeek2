Who is Hawkeye?

Hawkeye may not have superpowers, but don’t underestimate him! Known as the world’s greatest archer, Clint Barton uses trick arrows and sharp skills to fight alongside super-powered teammates. He’s loyal, quick-thinking, and surprisingly funny—even in the middle of a battle.

Fun Facts:

His hearing was damaged in battle, and in some versions, Hawkeye wears a hearing aid.

Known as the “ordinary guy” among superheroes—but he never backs down.

In the MCU, Hawkeye is played by Jeremy Renner.
