Who is Tony Stark?

Tony Stark is a genius inventor, billionaire, and superhero known as Iron Man. After a life-changing injury, he built a high-tech suit of armor to save himself—and later upgraded it to fight evil. His sharp mind, tech skills, and confident personality make him one of the most famous Marvel heroes.

Tony’s personality is witty, sarcastic, but deeply heroic at heart.
