Who is Thor?

Thor is a powerful Asgardian warrior and one of Earth’s mightiest heroes. Born as the son of Odin (the All-Father) and Frigga, Thor was raised in Asgard, a realm of gods and magic. He’s known as the God of Thunder because he controls storms and lightning using his enchanted hammer, Mjolnir.

Fun Facts:

Thor loves Midgard (Earth) and often protects it from cosmic threats.

Despite being a god, he has a fun, sometimes arrogant personality but a strong sense of honor.

In the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), Thor is played by Chris Hemsworth.

Did You Know?
Thor’s character is based on the Norse god from ancient mythology, but Marvel gave him a modern superhero twist.
